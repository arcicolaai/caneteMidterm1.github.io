About this Website

Our Mission

Our mission is to travel throughout the entire Philippines, explore new and fascinating destinations, meet new and interesting people, learn about the culture, eat lots of unique local cuisine, and have FUN doing so.


The purpose of this site is to share our knowledge and experiences of the Philippines and travel from the perspective of both local Filipinos and a well-traveled American/foreigner to help inspire you to discover and explore the Philippines… and have FUN doing so.


This site is organized to help you learn information about the Philippines, islands, cities, points of interest, and what you need to know more quickly and thoroughly to get the most out of each trip.

Our Goals are to:

  • Educate you about the Cagayan de Oro City BEFORE you go
  • Share our knowledge, expertise, and experiences that we’ve acquired over the past 3 decades
  • Build a community of like-minded people to share photos and experiences
  • Help you discover all the amazing things the Cagayan de Oro City has to offer
  • Inspire you to travel to and throughout the Cagayan de Oro City and explore and experience it’s wonders
  • Prepare you so that you get the most from each trip
  • Increase your knowledge and understanding to get the most from your trips and money
  • Decrease your frustration and hassles while traveling
  • Ensure your trip is an epic and unforgettable experience

      • We want to make certain you’re prepared for your trip, wherever you’re traveling to, and to ensure that you get the most out of every trip and create amazing memories.
